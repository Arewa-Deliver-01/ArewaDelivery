Refund & Cancellation Policy

We aim for fair outcomes. This policy explains when refunds or cancellations are available.

Order Cancellations

Customers may cancel orders before vendor acceptance. Fees may apply for late cancellations.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be issued for undelivered items, incorrect items, or verified service failures. Contact support to initiate a claim.

Process

Submit a report via Report an Issue or Support. We will investigate and contact you within 48–72 hours.
