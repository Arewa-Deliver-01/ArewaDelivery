Frequently Asked Questions

Your top questions, answered clearly and simply.

What is Arewadeliver?+

Arewadeliver is a modern delivery platform that connects customers, vendors, and riders. You can order items, deliver packages, or manage your business — all in one place.

How do I place an order?+

Simply create an account as a customer, browse available vendors or services, and submit your order. A rider will handle the pickup and delivery.

How can riders join the platform?+

Riders can register on the rider signup page, upload required information, and once approved — they can start accepting delivery requests.

Is my data secure?+

Yes. We follow best practices to encrypt and protect user information across the platform.

How do I contact support?+

You can reach our support team anytime through the contact page or support form. We respond as quickly as possible.
